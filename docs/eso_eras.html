Esophagectomy ERAS

Early Recovery after Esophagectomy

ERAS society guidelines (Low et al. 2019).

Systmatic review 2014 (Findlay et al. 2014)

Meta-analysis (Pisarska et al. 2017), included 2042 patients (1058 in an ERAS group vs 984 in a traditional group), revealed a significantly shorter hospital stay and fewer non-surgical and pulmonary complications in the ERAS group, but no effect on overall morbidity, mortality or readmission rates.
